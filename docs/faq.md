# FAQ

## Is Ved a general-purpose programming language?

No. Ved focuses on control-plane orchestration rather than application logic.

## Does Ved replace Kubernetes or Terraform?

Ved explores a different programming model for expressing orchestration
behaviour. Integration with existing tools is expected.

## Is Ved production ready?

Not yet. The project is in early design and prototyping stages.

## Why deterministic execution?

Determinism improves reproducibility, debugging, and operational confidence
in long-running distributed systems.

## How can I contribute?

Open discussions or issues in the relevant repositories to participate in
design and implementation work.
